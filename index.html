About

Fish Off the List was designed so that fish from the Monterey Bay Aquarium recommended fish list could be sold at market without interferring with the actual population of the fish.

This would allow for people to enjoy the fish that are in danger of being OVER fished but still not hurting the fish populace. The company uses sustainable and healthy methods to create the mass production of fish.

We here at Fish Off the List are interested in helping the population enjoy great tasting fish but not endangering the planet. We invite you to explore the site and see exactly what Fish Off the List has to offer you and your dining needs.
